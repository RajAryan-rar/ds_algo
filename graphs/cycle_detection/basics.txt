Summary Table

Method 	            Best Used For	            Key Concept

DFS	                Directed & Undirected	    Back edges, recursion stack (directed), parent tracking (undirected)
Topological Sort	Directed (DAGs)	            In-degree count, Kahn's Algorithm (BFS)
DSU	                Undirected	                Find parent representatives, Union-Find

---------------x-------------------x--------------------------x------------------------------x---------------------------

Important Distinctions:

*For undirected graphs, DSU is often preferred for its efficiency in dynamic connectivity scenarios.
*For directed graphs, DFS (with a recursion stack) or Topological Sort (Kahn's) are standard.
